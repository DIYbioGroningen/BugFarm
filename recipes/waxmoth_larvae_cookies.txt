#Cookies with white chocolate and waxmoth larvae (~36 cookies)

##Ingredients:

- 1 2/3  cup flour
- ¾ teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon baking soda 
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup soft butter
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- 1/3 cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 large egg
- 2 cups of white chocolate chips
- ¾ cup (~375) frozen waxmoth larvae (defrosted)

##Instructions:

1) Pre-heat the oven to 190 degrees C or gasmark 5.
2) Mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a bowl.
3) In a separate, larger bowl, mix the butter sugar (brown and white) and the vanilla extract to a smooth consistency.
4) Mix the egg with the butter mixture and then gradually add the flour mixture. 
5) Stir the white chocolate chips into the batter and add half of the waxmoth larvae. Save the others for decoration.
6) Using a teaspoon scoop the batter onto a sheet of baking paper and push a few larvae into the top of each heap fpr decoration.
7) Bake in the pre-heated oven for 8-12 minutes, or untill the edges are golden brown.
8) Leave to cool down on the baking sheet for 2 min and then transfer to a grate to continue cooling.
